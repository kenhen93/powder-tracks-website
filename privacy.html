Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

PowderTracks ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

Information We Collect

Location Data

PowderTracks uses GPS location data to track your skiing and snowboarding activities. This data includes:

  • GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude)
  • Altitude and elevation data
  • Speed and distance measurements
  • Timestamps of your activities

All location data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to export or share your data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide skiing and snowboarding tracking functionality
  • To display your run statistics and history
  • To detect which ski resort you're at (via geofencing)

Data Storage and Security

Your skiing data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. We do not operate cloud servers or collect your activity data. You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time through the app settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your location data never leaves your device unless you:

  • Export your data (GPX/KMZ files) and share it yourself
  • Manually share specific runs with friends

Third-Party Services

PowderTracks uses Google Play Services and Apple Maps for GPS functionality. These services have their own privacy policies that govern their use of information.

Children's Privacy

PowderTracks is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (view all your runs in the app)
  • Delete your data (clear all data in app settings)
  • Export your data (GPX/KMZ export)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: support@powdertracks.com

No Ads, No Tracking

PowderTracks does not use advertising, analytics, or tracking technologies. We don't know who you are, where you ski, or how often you use the app unless you contact us directly.

Bottom line: Your skiing data stays on your device. We're not in the business of selling your information. PowderTracks is built by a skier for skiers, not by a data company for advertisers.
