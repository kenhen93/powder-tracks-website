Your data, your rules

Exporting to GPX is always free — on the free app, before you buy, and even if you stop using PowderTracks entirely. Premium adds conveniences like cloud backup and scores; it never puts your own tracking data behind a paywall.

Heads up — free means local. On the free app, your history lives only on your phone. If the phone is lost or broken, your data goes with it. Export a GPX copy now and then, or let premium's cloud backup keep an encrypted copy safe.

Storage add-ons — coming later

Cloud backup includes 1 GB — enough for roughly thirty years of skiing. A whole decade of history is only about 33 MB, so the space is hard to fill. If you ever do need more, extra storage is coming later: it'll run roughly a dollar per gigabyte, one-time — buy it once, keep it, never a subscription. The base app and its premium unlock will still be a single $19.99.
