Frequently Asked Questions

When will the app be available?

We're currently in development and testing. Follow our progress or contact us at support@powdertracks.com for updates.

Which ski resorts are supported?

Currently focused on Colorado resorts including Arapahoe Basin, Winter Park, Copper Mountain, Vail, Breckenridge, Keystone, Loveland, and more. We'll expand to other regions after launch.

Can I import my data from Ski Tracks?

Yes! Export your data from Ski Tracks as GPX or KMZ files and import them into PowderTracks. All your historical runs will be preserved.

Will it always be free?

Absolutely. No ads, no subscriptions, no premium features. This is a passion project built by someone who loves skiing, not a money grab.
